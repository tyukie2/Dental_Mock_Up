    Dr. Yelena Chuvashova, DDS

    Dr. Yelena Chuvashova moved to Washington from Russia in 2002. Upon graduation from the University of Washington School of Dentistry she was awarded Doctorate of Dental Surgery with honors and inducted into Omicron Kappa Upsilon, a dental honor society for individuals who graduate among the top of their class and display integrity of character.
    Dr. Yelena enjoys practicing compassionate, minimally invasive and technologically advanced dentistry, which gracefully combines whole heartedness, creativity and science. She provides dental treatment to adults and children at her private practice in Issaquah as well as various Public Health and Community clinics throughout King County.
    Dr. Chuvashova is a lifelong avid learner passionate about improving her skills. She takes continuing education courses and participates in advanced dental seminars and study clubs. She is very enthusiastic about patient education, preventive care, minimally invasive dentistry, biocompatible dental materials, implantology and aesthetics.
    Outside of work, Yelena treasures spending time with her family and friends. She loves going to concerts, traveling, hiking, mountaineering, sailing and practicing yoga. She enjoys being involved in her community.
